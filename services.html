We offer a range of packages which combine the following services


Sound

A PA system for your event is arguably the most important aspect of the production process. This is why we get it right the first time, everytime. This means you can focus on your event without the burden of technical tasks taking up your time.

Lighting

Lighting your show truly elevate it, creating an experience your audience will never forget. Our designs encorporate your vision, giving you complete peace of mind while we manage the technical details.

We have a wide range of equipment. This includes fixtures which can wash the stage with colour, project patterns and create excellent eye-candy effects.

Photography

We include photography in all of our event quotes as standard.

We can also record videos for later viewing or possible sales. We do not take a commision from this.

We can also provide services for broadcasting a stream to platforms such as YouTube.

Both photos and videos can be available raw, edited or both.

Pre-show setup

Our services extend even before the show has started.

Lighting timecode

This is where the visuals on the stage are pre-programmed before the show, so they look extra perfect on the day. This also means that performers can have more of an impact on the design of their show.

LED screen graphics

Custom graphics can be created for your show. This is subject to an LED screen / projector being present. We currently cannot provide this equipment.
